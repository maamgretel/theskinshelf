About The Skin Shelf

Your trusted destination for premium makeup and beauty essentials, where quality meets affordability and every girl deserves to feel beautiful.

Our Story

The Skin Shelf was born from a simple belief: every woman deserves access to high-quality makeup without breaking the bank. Founded in 2023, we started as a small online boutique with a mission to democratize beauty by making premium cosmetics affordable and accessible to everyone.

Our journey began when our founder, frustrated with the high prices of quality makeup in the Philippines, decided to create a platform that would bridge the gap between premium beauty products and everyday affordability. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a movement that has touched the lives of thousands of beauty enthusiasts across the country.

Today, The Skin Shelf stands as the Philippines' leading affordable luxury makeup destination, serving over 25,000 happy customers with a curated selection of 500+ premium products. We've built more than just an online store – we've created a community where beauty dreams become reality.

From our humble beginnings to becoming a trusted name in Philippine beauty retail, our commitment remains unchanged: to provide authentic, high-quality makeup at prices that make luxury accessible to all.
